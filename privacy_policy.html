At Morning Zoria LLC ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you visit our website or use our services.

Quick Summary: We only collect information necessary to provide our custom sewing and tool sharpening services. We never sell your personal data and only share it when required by law or with your explicit consent.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Project Details: Information about your custom sewing or sharpening service requests
  • Payment Information: Billing address and payment details (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Communication Records: Records of our correspondence and service interactions
  • Preferences: Your service preferences, special requests, and feedback

Information Automatically Collected

  • Website Usage: Pages visited, time spent on pages, and navigation patterns
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Location Data: General geographic location (city/state level) for service delivery purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

Service Delivery

  • Processing and fulfilling your service requests
  • Communicating about your projects and appointments
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries
  • Scheduling pickups and deliveries
  • Processing payments and managing billing

Business Operations

  • Improving our services and customer experience
  • Maintaining accurate business records
  • Conducting quality assurance and training
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance our website

Legal and Safety

  • Complying with legal obligations and regulations
  • Protecting our rights and property
  • Ensuring the safety and security of our services

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Payment Processors: To process payments securely
  • Delivery Services: For pickup and delivery coordination (name and address only)
  • Technology Providers: For website hosting and maintenance

Legal Requirements

  • When required by law, court order, or government request
  • To protect our legal rights or defend against claims
  • In case of emergency to protect safety

Business Transfers

If our business is sold or merged, your information may be transferred to the new owner under the same privacy protections.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure Storage: Customer records are stored in locked filing cabinets and secure digital systems
  • Regular Updates: We keep our security systems and practices up to date
  • Employee Training: Our team is trained on privacy and security best practices
Important: While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your information using industry-standard practices.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Correction

  • Request access to the personal information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Update your contact preferences at any time

Data Control

  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Object to certain uses of your information
  • Request a copy of your information in a portable format

Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of marketing communications (service-related communications may continue)
  • Choose how we contact you about your projects

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this policy.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • For significant changes, we will notify you by email or website notice
  • Your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
