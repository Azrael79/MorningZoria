Our Story

Meet the passionate craftspeople behind Morning Zoria LLC and discover our journey of dedication to quality and service

Welcome to Morning Zoria LLC

We are Sergey and Natalya, a husband-wife duo who loves working with our hands, creating quality items, and combining creativity with practicality. Our journey began years ago when we immigrated to the United States in search of opportunities for our growing family.

Natalya has been sewing for her family and friends since she was in high school, developing her skills and passion for creating beautiful, functional clothing and textiles. As her craft evolved and her need for sharp tools in the studio grew, Sergey learned the art of keeping cutting instruments in tip-top shape.

Now that our children are grown, we have time to hone and expand our skills. The idea to turn our passions into a business was born from the joy we receive from our work and our desire to share it with you!

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to a thriving family business

1990s

The Beginning

Natalya begins sewing in high school, creating clothes for family and friends while developing her passion for textile arts.

2000s

Immigration & Growth

Sergey and Natalya immigrate to the United States, focusing on raising their family while continuing to hone their crafts.

2010s

Skill Development

Sergey learns professional sharpening techniques to support Natalya's growing need for precision tools in her sewing studio.

2020

Morning Zoria LLC

With children grown, we officially launch Morning Zoria LLC, combining our passions into a family business dedicated to quality craftsmanship.

Today

Serving Our Community

We continue to grow, serving customers with custom sewing and professional sharpening services, always maintaining our commitment to excellence.
